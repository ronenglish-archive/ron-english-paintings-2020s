Exhibitions

Now You See It, Allouche Gallery, New York, New York, US, May 20–July 4, 2023.

Notes

This work appears in the online PDF catalogue for Ron English: Now You See It (Allouche Gallery, 2023).

Allouche Gallery’s catalogue and League OTO’s preview list the work as Marilyn Sipping Monopoly Grin and Mickey; the catalogue raisonné title used here is Marilyn Slipping Monopoly Grin a Mickey.

Allouche Gallery’s catalogue lists the work as 2022–2023; the catalogue raisonné date used here is 2023.

This work references Marilyn Monroe, Mickey Mouse, Dig ’Em Frog, Tony the Tiger, Cap’n Crunch, Count Chocula, Ronald McDonald, James Montgomery Flagg’s I Want You for U.S. Army poster, Leonardo da Vinci’s Mona Lisa, and Rich Uncle Pennybags / Mr. Monopoly.
