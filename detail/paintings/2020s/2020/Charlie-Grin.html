Notes

This work appears on Artsy’s artwork page as Charlie Grin, where it is listed as a 2020 original oil and acrylic on paper measuring 28 × 22 inches (71.1 × 55.9 cm), unique, hand-signed by the artist, with frame included.

Pop International Galleries’ POPaganda on Paper catalogue also lists the work as 28 × 22 inches, reversing the dimension order used in the current catalogue record.

Artsy lists the work as hand-signed by the artist; no signature is visible on the front in the available documentation.

The composition references Charlie Brown, the Peanuts character created by Charles M. Schulz.
